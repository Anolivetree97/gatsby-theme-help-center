# Add Member to Study

1. Go to **`Study`**.
2. Select a study and click the **`View`** icon.
3. Switch to the **`Members`** tab, then click **`Select Member`**.
4. Click **`Add`**.
5. In the **`Add`** window, select the members that you want to add to this study, then click **`Confirm`**.
